Mac and Beef

In this recipe, seasoned ground beef is browned and cooked with a convenient tomato sauce made with pantry ingredients. Then the macaroni pasta is cooked in the beef sauce. This easy dinner is reminiscent of the childhood favorite, beefaroni. It is fun, simple, and comforting.

Ingredients

  • 1 tsp olive oil.
  • 1 onion, chopped.
  • 1 red bell pepper.
  • 1 tbsp minced garlic.
  • 1 cup lean ground beef.
  • season with 1/2 tsp salt and pepper, 1 tsp paprika and chili powder.
  • 1 cup tomato sauce.
  • 1 cup water or broth.
  • 1 heaping cup dry macaroni.
  • optional; 1 cup spinach

Preparation Steps

  1. Add 1 tsp olive oil on a heated pan then add chopped onion, choppped red bell papper and minced garlic. Cook until soft.
  2. Add lean ground beef, salt, pepper, paprika and chilli powder. Cook until golden brown.
  3. Add tamato sauce, a cup of water or broth mix in a cup of drz macaroni.
  4. Cover on low heat for 20 minutes then add spinach(optional)
  5. Serve and Enjoy
