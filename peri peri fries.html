Peri-Peri Crispy fries

Peri-Peri sauce originates in Africa (with a Portuguese influence) and is traditionally a blend of African bird's eye chilis. The flavor is typically spicy, garlicy, tangy, and lemony. The Nando's franchise popularized the sauce and flavors, but Peri Peri simply means "pepper pepper" in Swahili.

Ingredients

  • medium-sized strips cut potatoes.
  • Peri peri spice.
  • 1 tbsp salt.
  • 1 tbsp paprika.
  • 1 tbsp garlic powder.
  • 1 tbsp onion powder.
  • cornflour.
  • cooking oil.

Preparation Steps

  1. Wash, peel and cut the the potatoes onto medium size strips.
  2. soak in ice cold water for 5 to 10 minutes.
  3. Strain the water and pat dry the potato strips.
  4. Season with salt,paprika,garlic powder and onion powder.
  5. Add conflour and mix everything well until well coated
  6. Deep fry on medium heat for 5 to 6 minutes until golden brown.
  7. Once done transfer it to wire rack.
  8. Season with Peri peri spice mix,toss it well.
  9. Enjoy with your favourite dip.
